FAQs


  1. How do I add/sell a book to my account?

    You need to click here and fill all fields so that students can find it

  2. Can I edit my profile?

    In order to edit your profile, click here, and you will be able to make changes. Notice that you are NOT allowed to change your student ID number or your username.

  3. Can I have two emails linked to my account?

    No. The system uses one email at a time. If you change your email, your new email will be matched to your account.

  4. How do I buy a book?

    Click here and follow these steps:

    • Enter the name/author of your desired book and hit the "submit" button to search
    • A list of books matching your search will appear. Click view to see the quantity of relevant books available for purchase and their prices and descriptions
    • After you choose your book, click buy and you will be able to contact the seller/owner of that book

  5. Is there any limit to the number of books I can sell?

    No. You can sell as many books as you want.
